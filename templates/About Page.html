Around the Block

"Around the Block" is a unique apparel company co-founded by Gabi McLaughlin and Joelle Anselmo, two seniors at the University of Maryland. The concept for the company was sparked by our observation of the distinct and recognizable house names in College Park's Old Town neighborhood. We saw an opportunity to showcase the character and charm of the area through fashion and decided to bring their vision to life in October of 2022.

Have an idea? Want to see something new?

Our Mission

Instead of using new clothing items, we source our materials exclusively from local thrift stores, making each piece of clothing sold truly one-of-a-kind. Our commitment to sustainability and reducing waste is evident in the decision to use repurposed garments, ensuring that each item carries its own story and character.

In addition to our environmentally conscious practices, we are dedicated to giving back to the community. Around the Block donates 10% of our profits to Sloan Kettering Cancer Research Foundation in New York City, making a positive impact in the fight against cancer.

With each purchase from "Around the Block", customers can not only show their love for College Park, but also support cancer research efforts and contribute to a noble cause.

Meet Our Team

Gabi McLaughlin

Gabi is a senior at the University of Maryland double majoring in finance and business management. She's passionate about bringing teams together to innovate. She continues to explore finance, entrepreneurship, and business management in and outside the classroom.

She's also been a part of the Business, Society, and Economy Scholars program at UMD. Here she focused on active learning and collaboration, and through group projects in Scholars, she has learned the value of bringing together diverse groups of people to problem-solve. Gabi also held the position of CFO at Kappa Alpha Theta, and acted as a mentor for College Mentors for Success. Additionally, she was involved in Smith Undergraduate Student Association, where she helped plan a leadership conference for Smith, as well as a virtual trip to New York companies for undergraduate students.

Joelle Anselmo

Joelle is a senior at the University of Maryland double majoring in journalism and business management. She's fascinated by human centered and big authority stories and is devoted to learning about making sustainable choices, especially in retail. When she's not writing articles in the middle of the night or conducting interview, she loves reading books, journaling, daydreaming about traveling and living abroad, running and hanging out with friends and family.

During the school year, Joelle took thought-provoking, multimedia journalism classes that fine-tune her technology skills with software such as Premiere Pro, InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, and more. Her interest in journalism has encouraged her to become more involved with the surrounding community, where she has served as the President of the UMD Chapter of the Society of Professional Journal lists in her last year of college.
